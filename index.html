How to add the FAQ section to my Elementor site?

You need to contact with our support team using the live chat option on bottom right corner. Please provide your site details where you want to use the template for reference.

How to Import the FAQ section to my site?

Our support team will provide you a .json file. Once you’ve received the file you can then go to Elementor editor of your page and from bottom navigation click on Add Template > Import Template. Upload the .json file and click Insert.
Best Regards,
WebExperts Marketing

How to edit the Contents of the FAQ Section.

Once you’ve successfully imported the FAQ Section Template on your site from Elementor editor view, you can then click on the FAQ Widget area then you’ll see a list of options available to you for editing.
